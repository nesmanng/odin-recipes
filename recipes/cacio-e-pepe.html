Cacio E Pepe

Making Cacio E Pepe is easier than you would believe. It only takes 15-30 minutes of your time and you'll have a great meal that will satisfy you the rest of the day!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Butter
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta Water
  2. Cook pasta
  3. Add Cheese and Pepper
  4. Serve Hot

Carbonara Recipe

Making Carbonara is easier than you would believe. It only takes 15-30 minutes of your time and you'll have a great meal that will satisfy you the rest of the day!

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Spaghetti
  • Egg yolks and Whole Eggs
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the Guanciale
  2. Grate the Parmigiano Reggiano
  3. Boil Pasta Water
  4. Prepare the carbonara sauce
  5. Cook the pasta
  6. Add Guanciale, Parmigiano Reggiano with Pasta
